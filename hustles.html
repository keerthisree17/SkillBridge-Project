Scam Awareness

Warning Signs

  • Jobs asking money before work
  • Fake Telegram recruiters
  • Unrealistic salary promises
  • No verified company details
  • Pressure to respond immediately

Safe Practices

  • Use trusted platforms only
  • Research companies properly
  • Never share OTP or PIN
  • Use secure payment methods
  • Read online reviews before joining
