About SkillBridge

Learn • Work • Grow

Our Mission

SkillBridge empowers students and job seekers with practical skills, career guidance and employment opportunities aligned with SDG Goal 8.

We believe every young individual deserves quality education, decent work and economic growth.

Our Vision

Creating a future where youth are skilled, independent and career-ready.

Skill Development

Helping students gain industry-ready skills.

Employment

Connecting youth with jobs and internships.

Economic Growth

Supporting sustainable growth and innovation.

Our Objectives

  • Promote skill-based learning
  • Support youth employment
  • Encourage freelancing opportunities
  • Provide career guidance resources
  • Raise scam awareness
  • Improve digital literacy
  • Support entrepreneurship
  • Promote SDG Goal 8 awareness
